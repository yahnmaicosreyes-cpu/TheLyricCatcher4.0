# TheLyricCatcher
Lyric Search Optimization to make it easier for my church's media team to search up songs when the worship team is playing a song that is not on the list of songs for the day. The goal is to have this app run on Pro Prosenter as an add-on

## The Problem
"Right now, your media volunteers are under a level of pressure that makes them want to quit. They aren't just clicking 'next'—they are expected to be mind-readers. When a song changes unexpectedly, they have to search through a database of hundreds of songs by title. If they don't know the name of the song, they're guessing while the whole church watches them struggle. This pressure doesn't just burn out volunteers; it creates a 'technical barrier' that makes visitors feel like the service is unorganized or unwelcoming."

## The Solution
"I’m building a bridge between the Worship Leader’s spontaneity and the Media Desk’s speed. It’s called the The Lyric Catcher. Instead of your volunteer panicking to find a song title, they simply type the first few words they hear into a search bar. Our AI logic doesn't look for keywords; it looks for the meaning. It identifies the song instantly, asks for a quick 'thumb's up' confirmation, and puts those lyrics on the screen in seconds."

## The Bottom Line (Retention)
"By reducing lyric lag and mismatch by 60%, we aren't just making the tech look better—we are protecting the worship experience. We’re keeping the 'welcome' warm and ensuring that your members stay engaged in the song instead of being distracted by a screen. Let's make volunteering at the media desk a joy again, and make sure every person who walks through those doors feels completely invited into the worship."

## A Quick Visual Concept of the "Magic"
To help the tech-savvy leaders understand why this is different from the search bar they already have in ProPresenter:

Keyword Search: If the volunteer types "I love You, Lord" but the database has "I love you Lord," or if they misspell a word, they get Zero Results.

Your Semantic Search: The volunteer types what they hear. The AI understands the context and finds the match even with typos or slight variations, cutting the search time from 60 seconds to 5 seconds.
